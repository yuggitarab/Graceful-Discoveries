## WHY I BUILT THIS

> "I'm not a celebrity. Not a therapist. Not a guru. I'm a mother of three who lived through it."

I created what I wish I had at 3am.

There were months I could barely get through a day. Standing on a train platform with my chest tightening, trying to look like I was holding it together. Lying awake replaying everything, comparing myself to someone who should never have had a place in my life.

I was gaslit, dismissed, and told I was overreacting, while my body was already telling me the truth.

The turning point came when I stopped waiting for someone to fix it. I used my background in investigations, research, and wellness to understand what was actually happening in my brain and body. And what to do about it.

Graceful Discoveries is the result. Not fluff. Not vague positivity. Practical, neuroscience-informed support that helps you feel in control again, whether you stay, or whether you go.

— Iesha.
