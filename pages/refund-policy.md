## Section 01 — Digital Products

Due to the nature of digital products (including eBooks, courses, downloads, and other online resources), all sales of digital products are final. Once access is granted or files are downloaded, no refunds, exchanges, or credits will be issued.

This policy helps protect our intellectual property and ensures fairness to all customers.

## Section 02 — Services (Coaching, Workshops, Events)

- **Cancellations:** Coaching sessions, workshops, or events must be cancelled at least 48 hours in advance to receive a full refund or reschedule.
- **Late Cancellations / No Shows:** Cancellations made less than 48 hours before a booking, or failure to attend without notice, will result in the session being charged in full.
- If we must cancel or reschedule a session, you will be offered a new time or a full refund.

## Section 03 — Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this Refund Policy is intended to override your rights under the Australian Consumer Law. If a product or service is faulty, not as described, or fails to meet a consumer guarantee, you are entitled to a repair, replacement, or refund.

## Section 04 — How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are entitled to a refund under this policy or under Australian Consumer Law, please contact us with the following details:

- **Email:** info@graceful-discoveries.com.au
- **Subject:** Refund Request – [Order Number]
