## ABOUT GRACEFUL DISCOVERIES

**What is Graceful Discoveries?**
Graceful Discoveries is a betrayal recovery platform for women who have been blindsided by infidelity, deception, or a partner who was not who they appeared to be. Founded by Iesha Taraboulsi, a mother of three from Gold Coast, Australia. It offers practical, neuroscience-informed resources to help you calm the chaos in your mind and body, understand what's happening, and reconnect with a stronger, clearer version of yourself.

**Who is this for?**
For women who are in the middle of it. Those who can't switch their minds off, who are replaying everything, questioning their own judgement, and trying to make sense of what doesn't make sense. You don't need to have made any decisions.

**Who is Iesha?**
Iesha Taraboulsi is the founder. A mother of three who lived through betrayal herself. She used her background in investigations, intelligence, research, neuroscience, and wellness to understand what was happening and built Graceful Discoveries to share that path with other women.

## GETTING STARTED

**Where do I start?**
- Tonight: download the free guide, Turn Off the Crime Board (free and instant)
- Full path: The Storm Within course
- Just need to feel understood: Take Back Your Power memoir
- Not sure: Book a free Banshee Rant call (15 minutes)

**What is the free guide?**
Turn Off the Crime Board is a free downloadable guide. Covers why your brain is stuck in detective mode, a 3am checklist that actually calms you, and a nervous-system reset you can do tonight.

**Do I need to have made a decision about my relationship before I start?**
No. Graceful Discoveries helps you get stable enough to think clearly, whatever you decide.

## THE STORM WITHIN COURSE

**What is The Storm Within?**
An 8-module self-paced guided recovery course. Includes: 8 guided recovery modules, 120-page companion workbook, private community, email support, lifetime access.

**How much does the course cost?**
A$497, one-time payment. Lifetime access to all modules, workbook, and community.

**How long does the course take?**
Entirely self-paced. No deadlines. Some women move quickly; others return to modules as needed.

**Is the course therapy?**
No. It is an educational, personal development course drawing on neuroscience research. Not a clinical treatment. Iesha is a certified neuroscience and wellness coach, not a therapist.

## THE BOOK

**How much is the book?**

## BANSHEE RANT CALL

**What is a Banshee Rant?**
A free 15-minute call to vent, release, and figure out what you actually need. No program pitch, no pressure. 30-minute and 60-minute sessions also available.

**How do I book?**
Book directly through the booking page. Free. Choose a time that works for you.

**Do I need to have completed the course before attending a retreat?**
Not necessarily. Requirements communicated when each event is announced.

## OUR APPROACH

**What does "neuroscience-informed" mean?**
Grounded in how your brain and nervous system actually respond to betrayal trauma. Things like 3am thought spirals, hypervigilance, emotional highs and crashes all have neurological explanations.

**Will I be told to forgive?**
No. The focus is on getting you stable and clear. The rest is yours to decide.

**Is this only for women leaving their relationship?**
No. Support is for you regardless of what you decide - stay, go, or still figuring it out.
