## About Graceful Discoveries

Graceful Discoveries is a betrayal recovery platform for women who have been blindsided by infidelity, deception, or a partner who was not who they appeared to be. Founded by Iesha Taraboulsi, it offers practical, neuroscience-informed resources to help women calm the chaos in their mind and body, understand what is happening, and reconnect with a stronger, clearer version of themselves.
